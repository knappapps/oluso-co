RE: Formal Demand for Repair Under New Home Warranty — [Property Address]

Dear [Builder/Contractor Name or Company],

I am writing regarding the home I purchased from [Company Name] located at [Property Address], which was completed/closed on [Closing Date]. This letter serves as a formal written demand for repair of the defect(s) described below, pursuant to the warranty provided at the time of sale and applicable state law.

1. DESCRIPTION OF THE DEFECT(S)
[Describe the defect(s) in detail: what is wrong, where it is located in/on the home, when you first noticed it, and how it has progressed or affected the property.]

2. DATE DEFECT WAS DISCOVERED AND REPORTED
I first discovered this issue on [Date] and notified [Company Name] on [Date] via [phone/email/in person]. [Summarize any prior communication, repair attempts, or inspection reports, and attach copies if available.]

3. WARRANTY AND LEGAL BASIS
This defect is covered under [cite the specific warranty document, section, or state statute, e.g., "the builder's one-year limited warranty, Section __" or "[State] Construction & Warranty Law"]. A copy of the relevant warranty document/statute is enclosed for reference.

4. REQUESTED REMEDY
I am requesting that you [repair the defect / replace the affected component / reimburse the cost of repair] within [15/30] days of the date of this letter. If a professional inspection or repair estimate has been obtained, it is enclosed.

5. NEXT STEPS IF UNRESOLVED
If this matter is not resolved by [specific date], I intend to pursue all available remedies, which may include filing a complaint with [state licensing board], filing a complaint with the Better Business Bureau, pursuing mediation/arbitration, or seeking legal counsel.

Please contact me at [phone/email] to discuss scheduling an inspection or repair. I appreciate your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

Enclosures: [List any attachments, e.g., photos, inspection report, warranty document, prior correspondence]
